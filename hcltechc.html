Wipro Limited, also known as Western India Products Limited is an Indian information technology company established by Mohamed Hashem Premji as 'Western India Vegetable Products Limited' in 1945 which was later abbreviated to Wipro. It was previously set up as a refined edible oil manufacturer in Amalner, Maharashtra. After Mohamed Premji's death in 1966 his son Azim Premji took in charge of the company at a young age of 21. The year 1980 marked the arrival of Wipro in the Information Technology domain.
Wipro Organization Heads
1) Azim Premji is the chairman of Wipro Limited.
2) Abidali Neemuchwala is the CEO of Wipro.
3) Rishad Premji is the Chief strategy officer in Wipro.
